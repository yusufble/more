sophomore year

My second year at Horizon Science Academy - Columbus High School was more challenging because I took a Web Design and Programming class. This time, the teacher showed us how to do things and explained what we needed to create on our own. She would show us a picture, and then we had to code that picture ourselves. At first, it was really hard for me because I had no idea what I was doing. However, as time went by, I improved in coding and became better at completing my work. Overall, it was a difficult but rewarding experience that helped me learn and grow.
